Wall Volume

Gross wall volume minus opening volumes (doors, windows). Result gives net m³ of masonry or concrete for ordering.
Gross Wall Area—m²
Gross Volume—m³
Opening Deduction—m³
Net Volume—m³
To calculate opening volume: multiply each opening width × height × wall thickness, then sum and enter the total above.

Example: door 0.9m × 2.1m × 0.2m wall = 0.378 m³. Two windows 1.2m × 1.0m × 0.2m = 0.48 m³. Total = 0.858 m³.
