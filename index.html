About Me

Dessinateur Métreur en Bâtiment

· RNCP40559 ·

I came to drafting the long way round — through over a decade on construction sites, working with my hands on everything from masonry to prefabricated bridge deck infrastructure. That background doesn't leave you. It means when I'm drawing something, I already know what it's like to be the person who has to build it.

Currently completing a Dessinateur/Métreur training at GRETA Est Bretagne, I work primarily in ArchiCAD and Revit, with a focus on BIM coordination and technical synthesis. I take the work seriously — DTU compliance, clean layer structures, drawings that actually communicate — but I don't take myself too seriously.

Based near Rennes. Bilingual French/English. Better with a set square than with small talk, but I've been known to manage both.
